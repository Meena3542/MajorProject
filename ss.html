Welcome to Reduce Student Dropout Rates Initiative

This project aims to tackle the critical issue of student dropout rates in India. Dropout rates are influenced by socio-economic and educational factors, with marginalized communities being the most affected. Our software solutions align with the goals of the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 to ensure universal access to quality education.

About NEP 2020

The National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 focuses on improving access to education and reducing dropout rates at various stages. It emphasizes equitable education opportunities and the integration of technology to achieve these goals. Our project aims to contribute to this vision by offering innovative solutions to address dropout rates effectively.
